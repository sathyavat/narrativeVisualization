Understanding Vehicle Emissions and Efficiency in 2024

An interactive slideshow by Sathyavat Chandran (CS 416)

In the rapidly evolving automotive industry, understanding vehicle performance and environmental impact is crucial. This interactive slideshow provides an insightful look at emissions, fuel efficiency, and test procedures for the 2024 model year vehicles. Our analysis aims to shed light on how various factors—such as vehicle make, fuel type, and engine specifications—affect emissions and efficiency.

Overview of Vehicle Manufacturers and Types

We start by exploring the different vehicle manufacturers and types in our 2024 dataset. This section provides a breakdown of the various manufacturers and their vehicle categories—cars, trucks, or both. This overview sets the stage for a deeper dive into vehicle performance and efficiency metrics.

Emissions by Fuel Type

Next, we analyze the environmental impact of different fuel types used in vehicles. This section examines the emissions produced by various fuels, highlighting which ones are more environmentally friendly. Understand how fuel choices affect both vehicle performance and the broader environmental footprint.

Impact of Transmission Types on Fuel Efficiency

In this scene, we look at how different transmission systems influence fuel efficiency. Explore the relationship between transmission design and fuel consumption. Learn why some transmissions offer better mileage and the engineering behind these differences.

Fuel Efficiency vs. Horsepower

Finally, we compare fuel efficiency with engine power. This section examines the fuel consumption of vehicles with different horsepower, providing insights into the trade-offs between performance and efficiency.
