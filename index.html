Understanding Vehicle Emissions and Efficiency in 2024

An interactive slideshow by Sathyavat Chandran (CS 416)

In the rapidly evolving automotive industry, understanding vehicle performance and environmental impact is crucial. This interactive slideshow provides an insightful look at emissions, fuel efficiency, and test procedures for the 2024 model year vehicles. Our analysis aims to shed light on how various factors—such as vehicle make, fuel type, and engine specifications—affect emissions and efficiency.

Topics to be covered:

Overview of Vehicle Types

This scene introduces the dataset and provides a breakdown of vehicle types. It sets the foundation for understanding the variety and distribution of vehicles analyzed.

Emissions by Fuel Type

This scene focuses on the emissions produced by different fuel types. It highlights the environmental impact of various fuels, providing insights into which fuel types are more eco-friendly.

Impact of Transmission Types on Fuel Efficiency

This scene analyzes how different transmission types affect fuel efficiency. It explores the relationship between transmission design and vehicle performance, offering a detailed look at efficiency metrics.

Fuel Efficiency vs. Horsepower

This scene compares fuel efficiency with horsepower, examining how engine power influences fuel consumption. It provides a nuanced understanding of the trade-offs between performance and efficiency.

Our narrative follows an interactive slideshow structure. You can interact with the data by hovering over elements for more details and using buttons to navigate through the scenes.
